Our suggestions

1.我们强烈建议您使用Google或edge在电脑端浏览我们的网页。
1.We strongly recommend that you use Google or Edge to visit our Wiki on PC.

2.如遇Google浏览器的安全提醒，可在确保安全的情况下在“详情”中选择“访问这个不安全的网站”。
2.If encountering security reminders from Google Chrome, you can select "Access this unsafe website" in the "Details" section while ensuring security.

3.在使用我们推荐的浏览器阅览Wiki时，请保持浏览器处于100%的最佳阅览比例。如中途更改了比例，如需回到最佳的浏览器比例，请刷新网页。
3.The optimal ratio of your browser (Google or Edge) is 100% when visiting our Wiki. You can get to the optimal viewing ratio by reloading the Page.

4.如果你坚持使用我们推荐之外的浏览器进行阅览，阅览效果可能会受到影响。此时你可以对网页进行缩放以达到合适的比例。下面的两张图为您展示了我们的Wiki在最有比例下的阅览效果。
4.If you are not accessible to use the browsers recommended by us, 100% (of your browser) may not be the optimal ratio. If you encounter this problem, you can scale the webpage to achieve the appropriate ratio. The following two picture show you how our webpages are like under a appropriate ratio.

5.至此，你已经可以开始阅览我们的网页了，但是开始之前，我们的网页开发者在网页中留下了一个很容易被发现的小彩蛋，希望你能找到它。
5.Now, you can solve most of problems when visiting our Wiki. But our web developer wants to inform you that he had left an Easter Egg on the website. Hope you can find it!
